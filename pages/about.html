About Us: Ginger & Paris

Welcome to The Golden Paws, where our love for dogs and breakfast come together to create a unique dining experience.

Inspired by our two golden retrievers, Ginger and Paris, our cozy breakfast spot is a haven for dog lovers and breakfast enthusiasts alike. From classic favorites like Buttermilk Fried Chicken and Waffles to our veggie-friendly All Around Veggie Pancake Platter, each dish is crafted with care and canine-inspired charm.

Our dog-friendly space welcomes furry friends indoors and outdoors, so you can enjoy your meal in the company of your best pal. Plus, a portion of our proceeds goes towards supporting local animal shelters, because every dog deserves a loving home.

Join us at The Golden Paws for a delicious breakfast and a celebration of the bond between humans and dogs.
